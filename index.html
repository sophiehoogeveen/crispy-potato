Hi! My name is Sophie Hoogeveen. I'm 16 years old, and I'm currently a grade eleven student at the Linden School. I was born in Changsha, the capital city of Hunan, China. At 13 months old, I was adopted! Now, I live in Toronto, Canada with my mom, dad, and two older brothers. My friends and family would describe me as friendly, inquistive and detail-oriented! And they're right: I'm pretty awesome. I also like reading, and recently, I've started to learn Mandarin!

I made this portfolio as a culminating project for my computer science course (ICS3U). In the course, I've learnt a lot about HTML, CSS, and Javascript, but there is still much more to learn! We've also learnt a little bit of Python. In particular, I really enjoy developing the user interface and visual elements of a website. I'm excited to learn more about front end development! Coding has been a great way to engage both the creative and logical sides of me. I look forward to my future projects!
